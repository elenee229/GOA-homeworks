today i'm going to share a recipe of a caesar salad,it's a really tasty and famous salad.

ingredients

  1. 1 large romaine lettuce heart (or 2 small), chopped
  2. 1 cup croutons (store-bought or homemade)
  3. 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  4. 1 clove garlic, minced
  5. 2 anchovy fillets (or 1 tsp anchovy paste)
  6. 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  7. 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  8. 1 egg yolk (or 1 tbsp mayonnaise)
  9. 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  10. 1/3 cup olive oil
  11. Salt and pepper, to taste

      12. making steps

      the dressing

      Mash the garlic and anchovy into a paste using a fork or the back of a spoon.

      Add the mustard, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and egg yolk (or mayo).

      Slowly whisk in the olive oil until the dressing emulsifies and thickens.

      Season with salt, pepper, and optional grated Parmesan. Set aside.

      assemble the salad

      In a large bowl, toss the chopped romaine lettuce with the dressing.

      Add the croutons and toss again lightly.

      Top with shaved or grated Parmesan cheese.
