what is crypto and why crypto?

"Crypto" is short for cryptocurrency, which is a type of digital or virtual money that uses cryptography for security. Unlike traditional currencies issued by governments (like the US dollar or euro), cryptocurrencies operate on decentralized networks based on blockchain technology.

benefits

  • Decentralization
  • Lower Transaction Costs
  • Fast and Global Transactions
  • Financial Inclusion
  • Transparency and Security
  • Ownership and Control
  • Programmable Money
  • Investment Opportunities
